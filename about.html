About Us

Our Story & Mission

Founded in 2024 by Mygo Mwenda and Lusekelo Mwambelo, Feed the Street Charity Organisation is dedicated to fighting hunger and restoring dignity to vulnerable communities in Lilongwe, Malawi.

Our Story

From Observation to Action

Feed the Street was started in 2024 after noticing that many people in Malawi, especially those living on the streets, struggle to find food and basic necessities. The organization was created to address hunger and provide support to vulnerable individuals who are often overlooked.

In the beginning, the founders started by preparing and distributing food to a small number of people on the streets of Lilongwe, specifically in areas like Bwandilo and Kanengo.

Over time, the initiative has grown, reaching more people, attracting more volunteers, and organizing regular feeding programs. Today, we continue to expand our reach and impact across Lilongwe.

"We believe that every person deserves to be treated with dignity, regardless of their circumstances."

Guiding Principles

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To fight hunger by providing food and basic support to people living on the streets and vulnerable communities. We are committed to serving those who are often overlooked, ensuring that no one in our reach goes to bed hungry.

Our Vision

To create a society where no one sleeps hungry and everyone is treated with dignity. We envision a Lilongwe where vulnerable individuals receive the support they need to rebuild their lives and reintegrate into their communities.
