What We've Done

Key Achievements

Meal Distribution

Served over 2,000 meals to homeless individuals and vulnerable families across Lilongwe since our founding in 2024.

Community Reach

Helped more than 1,000 individuals through our various outreach programs and support services.

Area Coverage

Conducted several successful outreach programs in key areas of Lilongwe including Bwandilo and Kanengo.

Volunteer Network

Built a dedicated team of volunteers passionate about making a difference in their community.

Real Stories

A Story of Transformation

During one of our outreach programs, we supported a young man living on the streets with food, clothes, and guidance. With continued encouragement, he was able to reconnect with his family and begin rebuilding his life.

From Streets to Family Reunion

This is just one example of how compassion and support can transform lives. Every person we help has a story, and we are honored to be part of their journey toward a better future.
