Our Programs

What We Do

We provide essential support to vulnerable individuals through targeted programs designed to meet immediate needs and restore dignity.

Monthly Program

Street Feeding Programs

Our flagship program involves regular mobile food distribution to homeless and vulnerable individuals in Lilongwe. We prepare and deliver hot meals, fresh produce, and hygiene kits directly to those who need them most.

  • Hot meals prepared fresh
  • Distribution in Bwandilo, Kanengo & other areas
  • Hygiene kits included
  • Dignified service approach
Essential Support

Clothes & Essential Items

Beyond food, we distribute clothing, shoes, blankets, and other basic necessities to help protect vulnerable individuals from the elements and provide comfort.

  • Clothing and shoes
  • Blankets for cold nights
  • Personal hygiene items
  • Seasonal appropriate items
Community

Community Outreach & Support

We partner with local organizations to host community feeding events, provide guidance and counseling, and connect individuals with resources to help rebuild their lives.

  • Family reconnection support
  • Resource referrals
  • Emotional support
  • Community integration
Emergency

Emergency Feeding Programs

Rapid-response support during crises — natural disasters, economic shocks, and emergencies — ensuring no community goes hungry in their darkest hour.

  • Rapid response capability
  • Crisis support
  • Flexible deployment
  • 24/7 availability for emergencies
Our Focus

Who We Help

People Living on the Streets

Homeless individuals in Lilongwe who lack regular access to food and basic necessities.

Vulnerable Families

Low-income families struggling to meet their basic needs and provide for their children.

Crisis-Affected Individuals

Those affected by emergencies, disasters, or sudden economic hardships.
