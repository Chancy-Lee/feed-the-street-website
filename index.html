Who We Are

A Community-Driven Mission to End Hunger

Feed the Street was started in 2024 after noticing that many people in Malawi, especially those living on the streets, struggle to find food and basic necessities. What began with preparing and distributing food to a small number of people on the streets of Malawi has grown into a movement.

Founded by Mygo Mwenda and Lusekelo Mwambelo, we now operate in areas including Bwandilo, Kanengo, and other high-need communities.
