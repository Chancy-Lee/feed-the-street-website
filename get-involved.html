Make a Donation

How to Donate

Your generosity directly impacts lives. We accept donations through multiple convenient methods:

  • Mobile Money Airtel Money / TNM Mpamba
  • Bank Transfer Direct bank deposit available
  • Direct Support During outreach activities

What You Can Donate

  • Money - Funds meals and logistics
  • Food - Non-perishable items, ingredients
  • Clothes & Shoes - New or gently used
  • Blankets - Essential for cold nights
  • Basic Necessities - Hygiene items, etc.

All donations are used to buy food, prepare meals, and support outreach programs.

Become a Volunteer

We operate mainly through dedicated volunteers from the community who are passionate about helping others. Join our growing network and make a real difference.
